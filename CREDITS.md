(`folder-image`) by Pictogrammers, licensed under the **Apache License 2.0**.

- Source: https://pictogrammers.com/library/mdi/icon/folder-image/
- License: https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

The glyph is recolored white and composited onto a gradient tile; no
endorsement by the original authors is implied.
